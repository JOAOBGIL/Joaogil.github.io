About

Experience-based Industrial Designer. Working across Digital and physical Products, a Spaces or a Brands, the User Centred methodologies employed allow me to deliver thoughtful products and meaningful experiences.

I am a team player, that thrives within a multidisciplinary environment by prototyping, collaborating and questioning the stakeholders involved to deliver not just products, but a deeply insightful and innovative process.

What I Do

My aim is to solve complex challenges that shape the way we live, work and play by designing simple, elegant, user-centric products and services for clients just like you.
