Design Assistant - Freelance

Reed Kram an Clemens Weisshaar are the owners of an industrial design consultancy based in Stockholm and Munich. Their work redefines cutting edge design. They have been referred to as “the vanguard of the next generation of digital designers” (FORM magazine) and “the poster boys of a new breed of designers” (International Herald Tribune). Their projects span from software development to process design, from product design to architecture. They explore the vanishing line between the digital and the physical, always remaining committed to “redesigning design”. stakeholders.

I have been following the studio for a year - either located in Munich, or joining occasionally on the studio missions, set-ups and assembly lines. With the team I have developed and expanded a set of design skills, experimented with ground breaking technology, worked to deliver presentations to clients, produced CAD, technical drawings and assembled products, being involved on projects from driver-less cards, image distorting sub-woofers, Smart Ceramics or IoT technical illumination, on a thrilling and fast paced environment that pushed on the limits of my practice.
