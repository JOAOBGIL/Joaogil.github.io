Welcome to the Internet of Bodies

Biocomputer Future of Health Institute was my MA Thesis project. I worked on a model that develops fictional prototypes to create collaborative experiences with the public to negotiate the impact of emerging technologies in health care. The process was guided by extensive research on post-humanism, synthetic biology and the quantified self with the collaborative support of different experts - Scientists, Designers, Policy Makers, Engineers and Ethicists.

The end result is a collaborative experience in which the public becomes participants by experimenting the fictional injection of the Biocomputer and interacting with tools that facilitate their feedback. By collecting different qualitative and quantitative feedback this project aims to push the boundaries of public engagement and consultation within key stakeholders.

Your body is the new Biocomputer. But what if it was hacked?
